PRE-PRODUCTION RESEARCH

Funnily enough, through a stroke of irony, this video came about while I was stressed, researching my original topic. One of my favourite artists is Pierce the Veil, the band who wrote the instrumental being used in the background of the video.

 

While researching visual themes and inspiration, the 100 Thieves CDL (Call of Duty League) team announcement video, featuring cinematic shots as well as a darker overall tone. The overall theme of this video is what people feel like when stressed.

 

In terms of pre-production, I have a camera as well as my laptop and a number of mobile phones with HD cameras and used those to my advantage throughout the production process.
